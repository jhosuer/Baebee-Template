About Baebee

Baebee started out in 2015 as a one-sided affair. A love story of the CEO’s
family. Just seeing that smile on his first born’s face erupted joy and the idea
of capturing the moment so it could be re-lived for a long time or forever.
You know they say, your first is always priceless. Since then, Baebee has
expanded that moment to capture loads of beautiful families.

What We Do

Firstly, we believe that babies are magical and everyone of them counts.
So we help bring their imaginations and beauty to life using still and
moving pictures, adequately captuted by the best of photographers
and videographers you can find around.
